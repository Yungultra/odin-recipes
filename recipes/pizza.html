Sloppy Joe Pizza

Description

Sloppy Joe Pizza is an easy ground beef dinner recipe using Pillsbury pizza crust and homeade sloppy joe sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 cup yellow onion - diced
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 13.8 oz Pillsbury pizza crust
  • 2 cups shredded cheese
  • 1/3 cup French's fried onions

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400F.
  2. Cook ground beef in large saute over medium-high heat with a dab of butter or margarine.
  3. Add diced onions to the pan with the ground beef while it's cooking.
  4. Crumble ground beef well.
  5. Once ground beef is fully cooked, drain excess grease from the pan and reduce heat to low.
  6. Dump ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, brown sugar and onion powder into a medium mixing bowl. Stir well.
  7. Pour sauce on top of ground beef.
  8. Stir until all of the ground beef is coated in sauce and leave the pan to sit over low heat.
  9. Press pizza crust out onto a greased 10 x 15 inch baking sheet.
  10. Place the pan in the oven and bake the crust for 8 minutes.
  11. Remove the pizza crust from the oven and spread the sloppy joe mixture on top.
  12. Sprinkle the shredded cheese on top of the sloppy joe mixture.
  13. Crumble the French's fried onions slightly and sprinkle them on top of the cheese.
  14. Return the pizza to the oven and bake until the cheese is completely melted and the crust is nicely browned. 6-8 minutes.
  15. Remove the pizza from the oven and slice.
  16. Serve and enjoy!
